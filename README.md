## 🔮 What I'm Building

### In Early Access

- **Local-first design, no subscriptions.** Runs on your machine, uses your own cloud API keys or local LM Studio for LLM support. No monthly fee nonsense. We hate subscriptions as much as Jason Voorhees hates noisy campers.

- **Infinite character summoning.** The Portal is powerful, but fickle - it won't let you choose who or what arrives through it. Instead, it draws from the entire multiverse of known fictional characters, only allowing you to limit summons to popular-iconic characters, the full catalog of ALL possible characters, or Novel (AI-invented originals) each time you trigger the portal.

- **Character Portrait Art (optional, but recommended).** Use your Nano Banana API key to generate fantastic character portrait art for any character you desire, or just drag your own images into the matching character's folder to populate it yourself.

- **Create and view RPG Sheets** for any character summoned. Export these to beautiful PDF layouts. Dark and light sheets supported.

### On the Roadmap

- **RPG Quest Mode** - Freeform narrative play using any summoned character, powered by your choice of AI provider and your choice of genre. Enable "Chaos Mode" and the AI will leverage your existing collection, injecting characters from your stash into your game.

- **Adventure Mode** - A structured 3-act experience with a stakes and doom counter, win and loss conditions, Relic awards (character and genre specific) that persist on each character for victories or defeats, a mini-map compass spatial-tracking system, and much more.

- **Genre Merging** - A system that shifts characters across genres and designs games based on any combination you can imagine. Dracula in a pulp swashbuckling adventure? Sherlock Holmes trapped in a cosmic horror? The portal doesn't judge. Good luck.

- **Roguelite Ability Unlocks** - Every few level-ups you choose new abilities designed around that specific character and the genre being played - you never know what's coming. These persist in that genre for future games with that character as they level up.

- **World Builder** - Create and manage custom quest scenarios (built with AI or manually), specific to each character and saved per genre.

- **Lore Enrichment** - Vector database integration for deep character knowledge absorbed from your own text, Markdown, and PDF documents.

- **D&D 5e (2024) Strict Mode** - Optional rules-enforced play with a character sheet that transmutes your character to the closest official D&D class, while leveraging a Hybrid class system for characters that stretch too far beyond core class rules.
